About the data

The AJC compared sale prices of residential properties that sold in the first three months of 2013 to their 2013 county values, using fair-market individual-to-individual sales, bank sales of foreclosed properties and other sales considered valid under state law. We eliminated outliers using standards set by the International Association of Assessing Officers.
