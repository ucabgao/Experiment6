Grading Buttons

Did you get the prompt correct? Grade yourself so Skritter knows when you should review it again.

  • Tap anywhere on the canvas to accept the grade and move to the next prompt.
  • Tap a different button to alter the grade. Tap once more to confirm your new grade and move to the next prompt.

Writing Prompts

Welcome to your first writing prompt! Do you know how to write the character for ? Try and write it from memory!

  • Tap and learn the correct stroke order.
  • Tap to reveal the entire character. Careful though, tapping the button will mark this prompt as wrong.
  • Tap to start over and write the character again.

Tone Prompts

What is/are the tones for Draw the contour for the tone directly on your screen! Here are possible tone patterns:

  • 1st: Draw a horizontal line.
  • 2nd: Draw a line from the bottom-left to top-right.
  • 3rd: Draw a "v" shape.
  • 4th: Draw a line top-left to bottom-right
  • Neutral: Just tap the screen.
